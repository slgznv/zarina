Text


If Claire had studied harder in college, she would have secured a better job by now. She often thinks about how different her life would be if she hadn't skipped so many classes. Her friends who were more diligent in their studies are now working in successful companies, and Claire sometimes feels regret. If she had realized the importance of education back then, she wouldn’t be dealing with financial difficulties today. Instead, she would be enjoying a stable career and higher income.

However, Claire understands that it’s not too late to make changes. If she were more disciplined now, she could still improve her skills and find a better job in the future. She has already started taking online courses, and if she continues with this dedication, she believes she will have a chance to advance in her career. Claire also thinks that if she had saved more money earlier, she wouldn't be struggling to pay for these courses now. But she remains optimistic, knowing that if she keeps working hard, she will succeed eventually.
Moreover, Claire reflects on her personal life and relationships. If she hadn't been so focused on short-term pleasures, she would have formed stronger connections with her family and friends. Now, she wishes she had spent more time with them when she had the chance. If she had balanced her time better in the past, she wouldn't feel so distant from the people she cares about. Still, Claire knows that if she makes an effort to reconnect with them, she will be able to rebuild those relationships.

In terms of health, Claire also regrets her choices. If she had taken care of her body and mind, she wouldn't be experiencing such high levels of stress today. She used to neglect regular exercise and a balanced diet, thinking it wasn’t important. Now, she sees that if she had maintained a healthier lifestyle, she would have more energy and mental clarity. Claire realizes that if she starts focusing on her well-being now, she will see improvements in the long term.

New words:


1. Secured - обеспечила бы

2. Skipped - пропускала

3. Diligent - прилежные

4. Realized - осознала

5. Dealing - справляется

6. Disciplined - дисциплинированная

7. Advance - продвинуться

8. Focused - сосредоточена

9. Experiencing - испытывает

10. Clarity - ясность
