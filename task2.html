Lesson 2

Reading

Task №1:


Identify the sentences in the text that highlight the importance of physical activity. Explain how these activities contribute to overall health.

Task №2:


Analyze how nutrition affects energy levels and mood according to the text. Write a summary of the main points discussed.

Task №3:


Find examples of small habits mentioned in the text that can improve mental health. Discuss why these habits are effective.

Writing

Task №1:


Write a detailed paragraph about your daily routine, focusing on how you incorporate exercise, healthy eating, and relaxation into your life using Present Simple.

Task №2:


Compose an essay that explores the interconnectedness of physical health, mental health, and nutrition, using the ideas presented in the text along with additional research.

Task №3:


Reflect on the challenges you face in maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Write a short piece on how you plan to overcome these challenges by implementing the suggestions from the text.

Speaking

Task №1:


Discuss with a partner how the balance of physical and mental health can be achieved. Use examples from the text and share your personal strategies for maintaining health.

Task №2:


Present to the class the key recommendations for a healthy lifestyle mentioned in the text. Include examples from your own life to reinforce your points.

Task №3:


Explain how hydration impacts overall health. Use the text as a reference and add your own ideas about how to encourage better hydration habits.

Listening

Task №1:


Listen to a podcast about fitness and compare the recommendations given with those in the text. Identify any similarities or differences in advice.

Task №2:


Find an audio clip where a nutritionist discusses dietary habits. Take notes on how their advice aligns with the information in the text.

Task №3:


Listen to an interview with a mental health expert discussing stress management techniques. Compare their tips with the small habits mentioned in the text.
