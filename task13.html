Lesson 13

Reading

Task №1: Critical Analysis:


Read the passage carefully. Analyze the impact of the merger of multiple universities on the development of Shakarim University. Do you think that combining universities with different specializations can enhance or hinder the academic environment? Justify your opinion with specific examples from the text and any external knowledge you have about university mergers.

Task №2: Contextual Vocabulary:


In the text, there are several complex terms related to education and institutions. Find the meanings of the following words based on the context and provide their definitions: •Infrastructure
•Qualified teaching staff
•Doctoral students
•Higher special education
• Research centers
• Qualified teaching staff

Task №3:Inference and Argumentation:


Based on the information provided in the text, infer the importance of Shakarim University's diverse academic programs for Kazakhstan’s future development. Do you think such universities are crucial for the economic and technological progress of the country? Provide an argument, referencing specific examples from the text and real-world situations

Writing

Task №1:Essay on the Role of Education in National Development


Write an analytical essay on the role of universities like Shakarim University in shaping national development. Discuss the significance of offering interdisciplinary programs (engineering, business, social sciences, etc.) and how these contribute to societal progress. In your essay, compare Shakarim University with other well-known global institutions.

Task №2: Research Proposal


Imagine you are an academic researcher at Shakarim University. Write a research proposal on how the university could develop a new interdisciplinary program that integrates technology, business, and social sciences to address pressing issues in Kazakhstan. Justify the need for this program with references to societal trends and academic advancements.

Task №3: Compare and Contrast Essay


Write a 350-word essay comparing Shakarim University to other major universities in Kazakhstan or Central Asia. Focus on aspects such as the variety of academic disciplines, research opportunities, infrastructure, and the overall educational experience. Discuss which aspects of Shakarim University might make it more competitive and attractive to international students.

Speaking

Task №1: Debate


In a formal debate format, argue whether large, multidisciplinary universities like Shakarim University provide better educational opportunities than smaller, specialized institutions. You will need to address potential disadvantages, such as lack of personal attention or overgeneralization of academic programs. Prepare for a 5-minute presentation followed by a counterargument.

Task №2: Problem-Solving Discussion


Imagine you are a member of the university’s board of trustees. You need to discuss the university's strategic plan for the next 10 years. How would you prioritize the development of Shakarim University’s infrastructure, research capabilities, and academic programs to ensure its continued relevance and global competitiveness? Discuss your approach in a 5-7 minute presentation.

Task №3: Role-Play Scenario


You are a professor at Shakarim University, and a student is considering enrolling in the engineering program. The student is concerned about the application process and the workload. Conduct an in-depth conversation where you provide detailed answers to the student’s concerns, emphasizing the opportunities and challenges of studying engineering at your university. Make sure to mention the program’s integration with other fields and the university’s focus on research and technology.

Listening

Task №1:Note-Taking and Summary


While watching the video about Shakarim University, take detailed notes on the following aspects:

1.Key historical events that shaped the university’s development.

2.The main academic programs and specializations offered by the university.

3.The role of Shakarim University in Kazakhstan's education system and economy.

After watching, summarize your notes in 3-5 sentences . Focus on the main ideas and organize them logically. Your summary should highlight the university’s contributions to education, research, and societal development.

Task №2: Critical Thinking and Application


While watching the video, pay attention to the university's strategies for fostering innovation and research. Afterward, respond to the following:

What specific initiatives or programs does Shakarim University have in place to encourage research and innovation among students and faculty?

How do these initiatives contribute to the university’s reputation in both Kazakhstan and internationally?

Reflect on how these strategies could be applied to other universities, particularly in your own country, and discuss the potential challenges or benefits of doing so.

Task №3:Discussion and Opinion:


After listening to the video, discuss the following points in pairs or small groups:

How do you think the merger of various universities has affected Shakarim University’s ability to offer diverse academic programs?

Discuss the potential impact of Shakarim University’s modern infrastructure on students' academic success.

In what ways do you think Shakarim University can enhance its global reputation? Be ready to support your opinion with examples from the video.
