Lesson 4

Reading

Task №1:Comprehensive Reflection:


Reflect on how the events of 2020 forced people to adapt both professionally and personally. In your analysis, identify three key areas in which individuals had to change their behavior. Use specific examples from the text to support your points.

Task №2:Cause and Consequence Analysis:


Explore how the text presents the psychological impact of isolation. What were the primary causes of this emotional strain, and how did people attempt to overcome these challenges? Analyze the effectiveness of the solutions mentioned.

Task №3:Vocabulary in Context:


Identify the bolded words in the text and explain how each word is used to describe the changes individuals and communities faced. Then, create a new scenario in which these words could apply to a different global event.

Writing

Task №1:Critical Essay


Write a critical essay on how the events of 2020 altered human behavior on a global scale. Include a discussion of both the professional and personal changes people faced, and use examples from the text to support your analysis.

Task №2:Personal Narrative


Write a personal narrative describing how you or someone you know navigated the challenges of isolation and remote work during 2020. Use at least five of the bolded vocabulary words in your narrative to demonstrate how these changes affected daily life.

Task №3:Research Paper


Research the long-term psychological impacts of prolonged isolation. Compare your findings with the experiences described in the text. Discuss whether the text accurately represents the broader mental health effects seen in global populations.

Speaking

Task №1:Debate


Hold a debate on the long-term effects of the shift to remote work. One side should argue that this transformation was positive for society, while the other side should highlight the negative consequences. Use examples from the text to support your arguments.

Task №2: Discussion


In pairs or small groups, discuss how the pandemic altered social relationships. Was the shift to virtual communication sufficient to maintain human connection? Each participant should provide examples from the text as well as personal experiences to support their viewpoint.

Task №3:Presentation


3. Prepare a presentation about the importance of mental health during times of crisis. Use the text as a foundation, but also incorporate additional research or personal observations to explain how people can cope with uncertainty and stress.

Listening

Task №1:Podcast Reflection:


Listen to a podcast discussing how different cultures responded to the challenges of 2020. Compare the ideas and coping strategies mentioned in the podcast with the ones in the text. Analyze which approaches were most effective in different regions.

Task №2:Expert Interview


Listen to an interview with a mental health expert about how isolation affects psychological well-being. After listening, write a summary comparing the expert's advice to the experiences and solutions described in the text.

Task №3: News Analysis


Find and listen to a news report that discusses how communities reintegrated after lockdowns. Compare the reintegration process described in the news with the one mentioned in the text, focusing on the psychological and social effects.
