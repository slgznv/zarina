Lesson 1

Reading

Task №1


Read the text and identify all sentences that use get used to, be used to, and used to. Explain why each form is used in its context.

Task №2


Summarize the challenges described in the text and how individuals adapt to them. Use examples of get used to and be used to from the text.

Task №3


Compare the text with your own experiences. Write about a situation where you had to get used to something new. Use complex vocabulary from the text.

Writing

Task №1


Write a 300-word essay describing how you got used to a new routine, such as starting a new job, school, or moving to a new place. Use all three forms (get used to, be used to, used to) at least twice.

Task №2


Create a detailed guide for someone moving to a new country, explaining what they should expect and how they can get used to new customs and lifestyle. Include words like adjustments, demands, and alien.

Task №3


Write a reflective piece on why it is important to have persistence when adapting to new environments. Use examples from your life or from the text, focusing on how people adapt over time.

Speaking

Task №1


Prepare a 3-minute speech about a time when you got used to a difficult or overwhelming situation. Use advanced vocabulary and focus on how you adapted.

Task №2

Discuss with a partner the process of adapting to a new environment. Share personal stories and include how you adjusted to the demands and expectations in the new setting.

Task №3: Presentation

Give a presentation on why moving to a new country can be daunting. Include examples from different cultures and talk about how people get used to new norms and expectations.

Listening

Task №1:Podcast Reflection


Listen to a podcast or TED talk about cultural adaptation. Take notes on how the speaker describes the process of getting used to a new culture. Identify any uses of get used to, be used to, or used to.

Task №2


Watch an interview with someone who recently started a new job or moved to a new country. Write down how they describe their adjustments and what they found most difficult to get used to.

Task №3


Find an audio recording of someone explaining a difficult experience they had to adapt to. Write a summary of how they got used to the new situation, and analyze how they managed to cope with the overwhelming challenges
