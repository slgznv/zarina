Lesson 9

Reading

Task №1: Comprehension Check


Read the text carefully, then answer the following questions:

•What positive effects of technology on society does the author mention?

• How have smartphones changed the way people communicate?

• Why might digital detoxes be suggested as a solution to some issues with technology?

Task №2: True or False Statements


Decide if the statements below are true or false based on the text. Justify your answers with information from the passage.

•Social media has only had positive effects on people’s lives.

• Excessive online time can contribute to stress.

• Artificial intelligence has no impact on health fields.

Task №3:Vocabulary in Context


Find and highlight the words “overwhelmed,” “reliance,” and “countless” in the text. Explain their meanings based on the context and create sentences using these words.

Writing

Task №1: Opinion Essay


Write a 200-word essay discussing "The Balance Between Technology and Real-Life Interactions." Consider both positive and negative effects of technology on personal relationships. Use at least two examples of the Present Perfect to support your points.

Task №2: Personal Reflection:


Write a short letter to a friend explaining how you have recently adapted your use of technology (e.g., social media, smartphones) in your life. Have you limited your screen time, or have you found ways to use technology more productively? Use the Present Perfect tense to describe changes you’ve made.

Task №3: Future Technology Predictions


Write a short prediction about how technology might change in the next 10 years. Use at least three examples of the Present Perfect to describe recent advancements that hint at future trends.

Speaking

Task №1: Debate Preparation


Prepare a 2-minute argument either supporting or opposing the idea that “Social media has done more harm than good.” Use points from the text and add your own examples.

Task №2: Discussion on Digital Detox


Imagine you are discussing digital detox with a friend. Explain what it is, why people might need it, and whether or not you would try it. Use at least two examples of the Present Perfect to describe the impact of technology on people.

Task №3: Personal Reflection


InTalk about a time when you felt overwhelmed by technology or social media. Describe what happened and what you learned. Use at least three examples of the Present Perfect to talk about your experience and the changes in your digital habits.

Listening

Task №1:Listening for Key Ideas


Listen to a short talk or podcast on the topic of social media and teen mental health. After listening, answer the following questions:

1.What are two main ways social media affects teenagers' mental health negatively?

2.What recommendations were made to help teens manage their social media use?

3.How has the situation with teen mental health changed over the past few years due to social media?

Task №2: Listening for Specific Information


Listen to a report or interview where experts discuss the impact of social media on mental health. Write down 3 statistics or facts shared in the audio. Then, summarize them in 2-3 sentences.

Task №3:Discussion & Reflection


Summarize their opinions and explain whether you agree or disagree with their points.

•What are some positive aspects of social media mentioned?

• What are the negative effects they discuss?

•Do they think social media should be limited or banned for teens?
