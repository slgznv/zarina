Shakarim University

Shakarim University is one of the leading universities in Kazakhstan, located in Semey. The University was founded in 1995 and got its name in honor of the great Kazakh poet, philosopher and thinker Shakarim Kudaiberdievich.

The University trains specialists in various fields of knowledge, including engineering, pedagogy, economics, natural sciences and information technology. Modern infrastructure, research centers and qualified teaching staff ensure the high quality of the educational process. Shakarim University trains a wide range of specialists in the fields of social sciences, humanities, natural sciences, engineering, agricultural sciences, business and law.

Shakarim University was founded in 1995 as a result of the merger of pedagogical, zootechnical, veterinary and technological universities. Today, the university celebrates a long and rich history, which was mentioned above.

It trains doctoral students in more than forty bachelor's degree specialties, several specialties of higher special education, about thirty master's degree specialties and five specialty specialties.
